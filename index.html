Database

Every submitted assignment row, live from Supabase.

Availability

Per-date working status and caps, shared across the team. “Assigned” counts jobs logged for that date; Slots = Max − Assigned.

Technicians

The roster the whole app runs on. Priorities come per job type: High End → HE priority, Low End → LE priority. Each technician exists once even if they appear in both priority tabs.

Sync priorities from Google Sheet

Runs automatically on app open (max once per hour). Pulls Priority HE and Priority LE, updates existing technicians and adds new names, deduplicated. Techs removed from a tab lose that tab's priority.

Add technician

Edit tier, region, and priorities inline — changes save on the spot. Deactivate instead of deleting to keep history intact.

Dispatchers

Manage the DSR list that appears in Daily Entry, the Meeting board, and every filter. Renaming a dispatcher also updates their name across all historical records so their stats stay in one place.

Add dispatcher

Deactivate hides a dispatcher from dropdowns and the Meeting board but keeps all their history. Delete removes them from the list permanently — their historical assignment rows remain in the Database under their name.
