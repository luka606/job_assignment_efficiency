The roster the whole app runs on. Priorities come per job type: High End → HE priority, Low End → LE priority. Each technician exists once even if they appear in both HE and LE tabs.

Sync priorities from Google Sheet

Pulls the Priority HE and Priority LE tabs, updates priorities and regions for existing technicians, and adds any new names (deduplicated). Techs no longer listed in a tab lose that tab's priority. The sheet must be shared as “Anyone with the link → Viewer”.
